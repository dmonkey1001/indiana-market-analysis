The IT Power Structure: Centralization & Agency Roles

Indiana operates under a primarily centralized IT model, with the Indiana Office of Technology (IOT) serving as the core service provider. However, major agencies retain significant influence over application-level decisions and strategy, creating a hybrid "hub-and-spoke" environment ripe for strategic consultation.
